


**PLAYER STATS**

	Vigor: Determines health
	Intelligence: Determines mana
	Dexterity: Jump distance/height, movement and speed. Higher number makes platforming easier
	Charisma: Provides a discount in shops
	Memory: Determines what spells the player can prepare. Some spells cost more memory than others

**ENEMY IDEAS**

			**GOBLINS**
	Basic enemies, faster but less damage and health
		
	Goblin Grunt: Basic melee goblin enemy
	Goblin Brute: Advanced melee goblin
	Sling Goblin: Basic ranged goblin
	Goblin Mage: Spellcaster goblin, advanced ranged enemy
	
	
			**ORCS**
	More difficult than goblins, more hp and damage
	
	Orc Grunt: Basic melee orc
	Orc Archer: Basic ranged orc
	Orc Shaman: Buffs allies and debuffs enemies
	
	
			**UNDEAD**
	More advanced enemies, often in large groups or have debuff effects
		
	Shambler zombie: Basic undead, more health but less damage
	Shambler skeleton Basic undead, hits harder but less health
