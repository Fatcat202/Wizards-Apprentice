


**PLAYER STATS**

	Vigor: Determines health
	Intelligence: Determines mana
	Dexterity: Jump distance/height, movement and speed. Higher number makes platforming easier
	Charisma: Provides a discount in shops
	Memory: Determines what spells the player can prepare. Some spells cost more memory than others


**SPELL IDEAS**

	*Cantrips*
		Starter spells, use no mana
	X	-Jolt: Ranged electric
	X	-Icicle: Ranged ice
	X	-Firebolt: Ranged fire
	
	*Utility*
	X	-Heal: Heal self, but lose mana until able to rest
	X	-Jump: Gain the ability to double jump
		-Shield: Gain the ability to block attacks
		-Teleport (Maybe): Gain the ability to teleport to one location within line of sight and within a range
		-Invisibility: Become invisible (but not invulnerable) for a short time
	
	*Stronger Damaging*
		-Shroud of (element): Enemies that hit you take damage for a short time
		-Dragon's Breath: Breathe out cone of fire
	X	-Wall of Ice: Create a solid wall of ice, can stick out horizontally from walls
	X	-Wall of Fire: Create a solid wall of ice, can stick out horizontally from walls or vertically from ceilings
		-Lightning Bolt: Bolt of electricity, can strike through enemies
	X	-Fireball: Ball of fire creating a large explosion, damaging many enemies in an area
		-Shocking Touch: Electrocute with a touch
		-Flaming Grasp: Burn with a touch
		
	*Elemental Spells*
		-Icy Breath: Coat a surface with slippery ice
		-Rain Cloud: Cause rain to fall in an area, putting out fires and making puddles of water
	
	*Unplaced spells*

**ELEMENT COMBOS**

	-Fire + Ice = Water

	-Water + Electricity = Charged Water

	-Fire + Water = Steam

	-Steam + Electricity = Charged Steam
	


**ITEM IDEAS**

	-Healing potion: Restores hp
	-Mana potion: Restores mana
	-Scrolls: Single use of a non-prepared spell



**ENEMY IDEAS**

			**GOBLINS**
	Basic enemies, faster but less damage and health
		
	-Goblin Thug: Basic melee goblin enemy
	-Goblin Brute: Advanced melee goblin
	-Goblin Slinger: Basic ranged goblin
	-Goblin Mage: Spellcaster goblin, advanced ranged enemy
	-Goblin Bat: Flying melee enemy
	
	
			**ORCS**
	More difficult than goblins, more hp and damage
	
	-Orc Grunt: Basic melee orc
	-Orc Archer: Basic ranged orc
	-Orc Shaman: Buffs allies and debuffs enemies
	
	
			**UNDEAD**
	More advanced enemies, often in large groups or have debuff effects
		
	-Shambler zombie: Basic undead, more health but less damage
	-Shambler skeleton Basic undead, hits harder but less health
